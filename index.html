What is El Shaddai?

EL SHADDAI IS ONE OF THE COVENANT NAMES THROUGH WHICH GOD THE FATHER, CREATOR OF HEAVEN AND EARTH, REVEALED HIMSELF TO ISRAEL. HE IS THE SAME YESTERDAY, TODAY AND FOREVER. (HEBREW 13:8)

IN HEBREW EL SHADDAI MEANS THE GOD WHO IS MORE THAN ENOUGH AND HE IS THE ALL SUFFICIENT ONE. THE KING JAMES VERSION TRANSLATED EL SHADDAI AS THE ALMIGHTY GOD. (GENESIS 17:1; 35:11)

IT IS THE AIM OF THE EL SHADDAI DWXI-PRAYER PARTNERS FELLOWSHIP INTERNATIONAL, INC. TO PROCLAIM TO THE WORLD THE GREATNESS, FAITHFULNESS, POWER, GOODNESS AND LOVE OF GOD TO PROVIDE RELIEF TO PEOPLE WHOSE HEARTS AND MINDS ARE BURDENED WITH PROBLEMS. TO HEAL THE SPIRITUAL AND PHYSICAL AFFLICTIONS OF MEN IN OUR TIME AND MOST IMPORTANT OF ALL IS TO PREACH THE GOODNESS OF SALVATION OFFERED BY OUR LORD JESUS CHRIST.

Mission and Vision

Our vision is to bring about a revival of the true Christian spirit in our Catholic faith, particularly among the El Shaddai DWXI-PPFI members and followers, and to raise up a true Christian family and a community of Spirit-filled, generous, loving, victorious, joyful, and united believers of God who care and share each other's burdens and blessings for the glory of God, our Heavenly Father, El Shaddai as described in Acts 2:42-47.

On the other hand, our mission is to share the Light of the Lord Jesus Christ, the Joy of our Salvation, and our faith in Him through our own personal testimonies of the power of His Word such as our conversion through repentance and deliverance from the bondage of sin and vices; the transformation from our old self-centered, destructive and confused lives to new victorious lives in Christ Jesus; and the healing of our broken relationships and physical ailments. We want to let the people know that God is alive, that He loves and cares for us and that He is willing to forgive us, heal our diseases, and deliver us from all our troubles if we repent of our sins, turn away from our wicked ways, and establish a personal relationship with the Lord Jesus Christ Who shall set our hearts on fire with the love of God and neighbor.

What plan of action does the El Shaddai DWXI-PPFI undertake to pursue its vision and mission? To pursue our vision and mission, we conduct fellowships and programs which are focused on the inspired proclamation of the Living Word of God and the life-testimonies of people whose lives have been touched by El Shaddai.

We also utilize the modern tri-media technologies: television, radio and print. In addition, community chapters nationwide and international chapters abroad hold weekly prayer-meetings as a result of the enthusiasm of the prayer-partners to share with others the El Shaddai DWXI-PPFI's Spirit of Faith, Love and Unity!
